# Client Hub Demo Video Script

**Total runtime:** ~8-10 minutes
**Recording tool:** Guidde (auto-narration), Loom, or OBS Studio

---

## Setup Before Recording

- Open `http://localhost:5001` (or your production URL)
- Make sure you're on the Dashboard tab
- Have the app in a maximized browser window

---

## Intro (30 sec)

**Narration:**

> "Running an insurance brokerage means juggling dozens of clients, hundreds of policies, and renewal dates that never stop coming.
>
> That's why we built Client Hub — a single platform where brokers track everything from employee benefits and commercial lines to personal insurance, COBRA, and billing.
>
> No spreadsheet sprawl. No missed renewals. No hunting for last year's outstanding items.
>
> In the next few minutes, I'll walk you through how Client Hub fits into a broker's day — managing clients, tracking renewals, generating invoices, and staying on top of every action item.
>
> Let's dive in."

**Actions:**
1. Open with the Client Hub logo / login screen, or a clean Dashboard view as the camera fades in

---

## Scene 1: Dashboard Overview (1 min)

**Narration:** "This is Client Hub — a centralized platform for managing insurance clients, policies, renewals, and invoicing."

**Actions:**
1. Show the left sidebar — point out the grouped sections: Clients, Individuals, Admin
2. Hover over each summary card (Total Clients, Benefits, Commercial, Personal)
3. Click on the "Commercial Policies" card — it navigates to the Commercial tab
4. Navigate back to Dashboard

**Narration:** "The dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your book of business with quick-access metrics."

5. Scroll to the Renewals chart — mention "12-month renewal forecast"
6. Show the month tabs (May, June, July) — click between them
7. Point out the "Starting from" date picker
8. Scroll to Action Items — show Outstanding Items tab, then Prospects tab

**Narration:** "Renewals and action items help you stay on top of upcoming deadlines and outstanding work."

---

## Scene 2: Clients (1 min)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Clients** in sidebar
2. Type a search term in the search bar (e.g., "Aptiva")
3. Click **Add Client** button — show the modal fields briefly, then cancel
4. Click the edit icon on an existing client — show the client details modal
5. Point out the contact fields, industry, status
6. Cancel out

**Narration:** "The Clients section lets you manage your client roster — add new clients, search, edit details, and track contacts."

---

## Scene 3: Employee Benefits (45 sec)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Benefits** in sidebar
2. Scroll the table — point out the coverage columns (Medical, Dental, Vision, etc.)
3. Click edit on a row — show the Benefits modal
4. Show the tabbed coverage types (Med, Dental, Vision, Life, LTD, etc.)
5. Point out carrier, renewal date, and outstanding item fields
6. Cancel out

**Narration:** "Each client's employee benefits are tracked with carrier details, renewal dates, and outstanding items across all plan types."

---

## Scene 4: Commercial Insurance (1 min)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Commercial** in sidebar
2. Show the Coverage column chips (WC, GL, Auto, etc.)
3. Show the Products column (e.g., "3 / 17")
4. Click edit on **Aptiva Corp** — show the Commercial modal
5. Click through a few coverage tabs (GL, BOP, Umbrella)
6. Show carrier, limits, premium, renewal date fields
7. Show the **Generate Invoice** button at the bottom of the modal

**Narration:** "Commercial insurance tracks all coverage types with detailed policy information. You can generate invoices directly from here."

---

## Scene 5: Generate Invoice (1.5 min)

**Actions:**
1. From the Aptiva commercial modal, click **Generate Invoice**
2. Show the policy checkboxes with premiums listed
3. Check/uncheck a few policies
4. Point out the subtotal updating
5. Check the **Binder Invoice** checkbox — show the 25% calculation
6. Point out the subject line changing to "Binder Invoice"
7. Uncheck Binder — show it revert to full premium
8. Fill in the email fields (To, CC)
9. Click **Preview** — show the PDF opening in a new tab
10. Close the PDF tab
11. Point out the **Send** button (don't actually send)
12. Cancel out of the invoice dialog and commercial modal

**Narration:** "Invoices can be generated for any combination of coverages. The binder option charges 25% as a deposit. Preview before sending to verify."

---

## Scene 6: Invoices Page (1 min)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Invoices** in sidebar
2. Show the All Invoices / Pending tabs
3. Point out the Type column (Annual vs Binder chips)
4. Hover over the coverage pills — show policy number tooltips
5. Show the month filter — select a month, then click "All"
6. Click **Paid** on a pending invoice — fill in payment date, add a note, confirm
7. Show the status change to "Paid"
8. Click **Undo** on the paid invoice — confirm in dialog
9. Click **Void** on a pending invoice — enter a reason, confirm
10. Show the "Voided" status with strikethrough

**Narration:** "The Invoices page tracks all sent invoices with payment status. You can record payments, undo mistakes, or void invoices."

---

## Scene 7: Individuals & Personal Insurance (45 sec)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Individuals** in sidebar
2. Show the list, point out the search
3. Click **Personal** in sidebar
4. Show a personal insurance record — point out Auto, Homeowners, Umbrella sections

**Narration:** "Individual clients and their personal insurance policies are managed separately from commercial accounts."

---

## Scene 8: COBRA Management (45 sec)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Cobra** in sidebar
2. Show the All / Active / Terminated tabs
3. Click **Add Coverage** — fill in first name, last name
4. Select a client from the dropdown
5. Select a state
6. Set start and end dates
7. Click Add
8. Show the new record in the list
9. Click **Terminate** on it — enter a date and reason, confirm
10. Show the status change to "Terminated"
11. Click **Undo** — confirm — show it reactivate

**Narration:** "COBRA coverages are tracked with start and end dates, linked to clients. Terminations are logged with reasons and can be reversed."

---

## Scene 9: Import & Export (1 min)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Export** button in the header
2. Show the downloaded Excel file briefly (mention 7 sheets: Clients, Individuals, Benefits, Commercial, Personal, Invoices, Cobra)
3. Click **Import** button in the header
4. Select the exported file
5. Show the import progress
6. Show the success message with record counts

**Narration:** "Data can be exported to Excel for offline review and imported back. The system validates the file structure before importing and logs any errors."

---

## Scene 10: Chat Assistant (30 sec)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Chat** in sidebar
2. Type: "Which clients have GL renewals next month?"
3. Wait for the AI response
4. Type: "How many commercial policies do we have?"
5. Show the response

**Narration:** "The built-in AI assistant can answer questions about your data — renewals, policy counts, client lookups, and more."

---

## Closing (15 sec)

**Narration:** "That's Client Hub — your all-in-one platform for managing insurance clients, policies, invoicing, and COBRA administration. Thank you for watching."

---

## Recording Tips

- Use Guidde for auto-narration, or Loom with your own voiceover
- Move slowly between clicks so the viewer can follow
- Pause 2 seconds on each new screen before clicking
- Use a clean dataset (the current mock data works well)
